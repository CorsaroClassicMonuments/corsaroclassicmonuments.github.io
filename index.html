Our Story

Dan and Candice Corsaro are local owners and operators. Certified in monument restoration and conservation, and as proud members of the Ontario Monument Builders Association, we bring compassion and professionalism to every project - honoring and commemorating loved ones with care.

Corsaro Classic Engraving is committed to quality craftsmanship, using durable granite and other lasting materials built to stand the test of time. We proudly support Canadian sourced granite.

What We Offer

We provide a wide range of custom monument designs, lettering, and on-site restoration services.

Cemetery Memorials

Custom monuments, markers, granite benches, and plaques in granite, bronze, and corian.

Accessories

Granite and metal vases, statues, memorial lamps, and UV protected ceramic photos.

Specialty Stones

Garden and pet memorial rocks, address stones, and custom boulders.

On-Site Services

Monument design, on-site lettering/inscriptions, restoration, and cleaning.

*Note: We do not sandblast on shaling (peeling/crumbling) rocks.
